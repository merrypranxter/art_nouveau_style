# PROMPT_PACK.md

Reusable prompt templates aligned to `STYLE_SPEC.md`.

## Global style prefix
Use this prefix for all prompts:

> Art Nouveau poster aesthetic in the spirit of Le Style Mucha; flowing whiplash curves, ornamental botanical framing, elegant linework, stylized figure and drapery, muted jewel-and-earth palette, decorative flatness with subtle lithographic texture.

## Template A — Portrait Poster
**Prompt**
`[GLOBAL PREFIX] A [subject] shown in [pose], centered within a [halo/arch/medallion] frame, surrounded by [motifs], with [palette], intricate border ornaments, period poster composition, high design coherence.`

**Negative prompt**
`photorealism, modern glossy 3D render, sci-fi UI overlays, random clutter, harsh HDR, neon cyberpunk palette`

## Template B — Symbolic Emblem Panel
**Prompt**
`[GLOBAL PREFIX] A symbolic panel representing [theme], featuring [primary emblem], radial ornament, intertwined vines and flowers, geometric border accents, balanced asymmetry, refined vintage print finish.`

**Negative prompt**
`flat clipart look, modern logo minimalism, low-detail background, chaotic composition`

## Template C — Narrative Scene
**Prompt**
`[GLOBAL PREFIX] A narrative scene of [subject/action] in a decorative environment with layered botanical motifs, flowing hair/fabric arcs, integrated title cartouche space, painterly-poster hybrid finish, cohesive Art Nouveau design language.`

**Negative prompt**
`cinematic realism, lens flare, contemporary clothing cues, hard-edged industrial design`

## Iteration knobs
- Increase ornament density: “more filigree borders, denser botanical curls”.
- Simplify composition: “fewer motifs, stronger silhouette, clearer negative space”.
- Push historical tone: “aged paper, early-20th-century lithograph print texture”.
