# Palette System

Approved palettes for Art Nouveau work. Each palette includes hex values, contrast behavior, and ideal usage context.

## 1) Dawn Allegory
- **Hex set:** `#F6D7A7`, `#EFA76B`, `#C66A4A`, `#6D7F5E`, `#2F3A3A`
- **Contrast notes:** Warm light base with medium-dark anchors; ensure text uses `#2F3A3A` or darker.
- **Use context:** Sunrise scenes, themes of awakening, optimistic title cards.

## 2) Nocturne Velvet
- **Hex set:** `#0F1B2E`, `#23395B`, `#5A4E7C`, `#B8A1D9`, `#E7DFF6`
- **Contrast notes:** High dark-field contrast; reserve pale lilac for highlights only.
- **Use context:** Moonlit narratives, dream states, introspective posters.

## 3) Spring Allegory
- **Hex set:** `#EAF4D8`, `#C7E59A`, `#89B56A`, `#5F7F5B`, `#A86B7B`
- **Contrast notes:** Low-to-medium contrast; add deep stem green for readability in dense ornament.
- **Use context:** Renewal, botanical studies, feminine portrait work.

## 4) Antique Gold & Ivory
- **Hex set:** `#F3E8CF`, `#D9C38A`, `#B08A45`, `#6E5A3B`, `#2B2520`
- **Contrast notes:** Strong value ladder from ivory to near-black; ideal for ornate borders.
- **Use context:** Heritage branding, archival facsimiles, ceremonial compositions.

## 5) Verdigris Conservatory
- **Hex set:** `#D7E6DF`, `#95B8AA`, `#5F8C80`, `#3F5F5A`, `#2B3C3A`
- **Contrast notes:** Cool tonal compression; use dark teal lines to avoid washed forms.
- **Use context:** Glasshouse flora, scholarly charts, restrained luxury layouts.

## 6) Rose Opera
- **Hex set:** `#F7E3E7`, `#E9B7C2`, `#C7778F`, `#7B4A5B`, `#33262D`
- **Contrast notes:** Midtone-heavy; include `#33262D` linework for stable edges.
- **Use context:** Romantic narratives, theatrical posters, perfume-inspired identities.

## 7) Amber Harvest
- **Hex set:** `#F9E1B5`, `#E8B35A`, `#C57A2A`, `#8D4F1E`, `#3B281D`
- **Contrast notes:** Saturated warm spectrum; avoid large `#C57A2A` areas without cool counterbalance.
- **Use context:** Late-summer abundance, artisan food labels, folk-ornament hybrids.

## 8) Mist & Slate
- **Hex set:** `#E5E8EB`, `#BAC2C9`, `#8E99A4`, `#5B6672`, `#2A3138`
- **Contrast notes:** Reliable neutral contrast for typography-heavy compositions.
- **Use context:** Editorial layouts, monochrome studies, structural ornament drafts.

## 9) Peacock Jewel
- **Hex set:** `#0D3B48`, `#146C7A`, `#1B8E8A`, `#6BAF8E`, `#E3C46B`, `#F2E7C9`
- **Contrast notes:** High chroma accents should stay below 20% of total area.
- **Use context:** Festive panels, peacock motifs, statement medallions.

## Usage Rules
- Limit active palette to **5–6 simultaneous colors** in a single panel.
- Keep linework at one of the two darkest values in the chosen palette.
- Reserve the brightest value for small focal accents (jewelry, stars, eye highlights).
- If accessibility is required, target text/background contrast equivalent to WCAG AA where possible.
