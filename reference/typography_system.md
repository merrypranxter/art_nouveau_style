# Typography System (Period-Compatible)

## 1) Hierarchy Model

- **H1 (Poster Title):** decorative serif/display, high contrast allowed, tightly integrated with frame.
- **H2 (Subtitle / Event Info):** calmer serif with wider tracking.
- **H3 (Credits / Footer):** compact serif, reduced ornament, high legibility.

## 2) Placement Rules

- Top title band height: **10–16%** of total composition.
- Footer info band height: **8–14%**.
- Maintain at least **2x line-height** spacing between title ornaments and figure silhouette.
- Keep text off high-noise zones (dense foliage, filigree knots).

## 3) Ornament Coupling

- Couple initial capitals with vine curls or bead terminals.
- Repeat one motif from artwork in text separators (e.g., dot, fleuron, petal).
- Avoid fully detached text blocks; connect with ribbons, arches, or cartouches.

## 4) Tracking & Case Heuristics

- Titles in uppercase small caps or title case.
- Suggested tracking:
  - H1: +20 to +60 (display dependent)
  - H2: +10 to +30
  - H3: +5 to +20
- Avoid condensed modern grotesk behavior.

## 5) Prompt Clauses for Text-Safe Layouts

- `reserved top cartouche with clean interior for title lettering`
- `lower information panel with minimal ornament and strong value contrast`
- `typography zones remain uncluttered and frame-locked`
