# MOTIF_DICTIONARY.md

Reference motifs and intent tags for consistent Art Nouveau prompting.

## Botanical motifs
- **Iris / lily:** elegance, vertical grace, spiritual refinement.
- **Poppy:** dreamlike mood, softness, poetic melancholy.
- **Rose:** romance, ideal beauty, decorative fullness.
- **Vines/tendrils:** movement, continuity, compositional flow.
- **Ginkgo/laurel leaves:** memory, honor, classical-natural fusion.

## Structural motifs
- **Halo disc:** sanctified focal emphasis around head/emblem.
- **Arch niche:** stage-like framing and vertical hierarchy.
- **Medallion/cartouche:** title/insignia container.
- **Sunburst/radial lines:** symbolic illumination and rhythm.
- **Border filigree:** edge containment and decorative cadence.

## Figure motifs
- **Flowing hair ribbons:** primary curve engine in composition.
- **Draped garments:** secondary wave system; adds grace.
- **Profile pose:** classical elegance and poster readability.
- **Frontal icon pose:** monumentality and symbolic clarity.

## Texture + finish motifs
- **Lithographic grain:** period print authenticity.
- **Aged paper patina:** archival warmth.
- **Muted metallic accents:** premium decorative hierarchy.

## Anti-motifs (avoid)
- Chrome/plastic surfaces, hard sci-fi geometry, modern UI glyphs, overblown cinematic realism, random fantasy armor unless fully stylized into Nouveau language.
