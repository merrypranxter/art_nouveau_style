# Complete Example Prompts (with Motif Mapping)

Below are 10 complete examples spanning portrait, poster, decorative panel, and allegorical scene use cases.

---

## 1) Iris Empress Portrait
**Prompt**  
`A regal woman in three-quarter view, Art Nouveau portrait, flowing auburn hair interwoven with iris blossoms, elongated elegant silhouette, luminous peacock-blue and ivory palette, ornamental halo with gold filigree arcs, delicate lithographic texture, soft diffused studio light, decorative botanical border, turn-of-the-century illustration style, high harmony, 4:5 composition`  
**Rationale**: Establishes iconic feminine portrait language with floral identity and period texture.  
**Motif mapping**: `Iris = nobility/wisdom`, `halo arcs = spiritual elegance`.

## 2) Absinthe House Poster
**Prompt**  
`Art Nouveau advertising poster for an absinthe house, central dancing figure holding a coupe glass, dramatic arabesque smoke ribbons forming frame lines, limited palette of jade green, cream, and antique gold, empty cartouche at top for title text, flat decorative color planes, subtle paper grain, vintage lithograph print look, clean silhouette readability, vertical 2:3`  
**Rationale**: Optimized for poster hierarchy and negative space for typography.  
**Motif mapping**: `Smoke ribbons = intoxication/flow`, `jade palette = absinthe identity`.

## 3) Ginkgo Decorative Panel
**Prompt**  
`Decorative Art Nouveau panel featuring a standing figure wrapped in cascading ginkgo leaves, near-symmetrical composition, flowing contour lines merging figure and foliage, enamel-like flat tones with muted amber and olive accents, mosaic-inspired background geometry, ornate inner frame, handcrafted wall panel aesthetic, soft ambient lighting, 3:4 aspect ratio`  
**Rationale**: Targets collectible decorative object feel over narrative realism.  
**Motif mapping**: `Ginkgo = longevity/resilience`, `symmetry = architectural calm`.

## 4) Allegory of Dawn
**Prompt**  
`Allegorical Art Nouveau scene of Dawn personified as a radiant maiden lifting a veil of night, swallows and poppies circling in whiplash curves, layered pastel sky bands in peach, lilac, and pale gold, elegant linework, symbolic sun-disc behind figure, decorative border with corner emblems, dreamy narrative clarity, poster-illustration fusion, 16:10`  
**Rationale**: Balances symbolism with readable storytelling.  
**Motif mapping**: `Swallows = renewal`, `sun-disc = awakening`, `poppies = transition/sleep to day`.

## 5) Winter Tea Salon Poster
**Prompt**  
`Art Nouveau winter tea salon poster, seated woman in flowing cream gown pouring tea, steam forms sinuous ornamental curls, pine and holly motifs integrated into frame, restrained palette of teal, cream, crimson accents, blank oval medallion for brand mark, lithographic print texture, decorative yet legible composition, 2:3 vertical`  
**Rationale**: Seasonal commercial prompt with controlled ornament density.  
**Motif mapping**: `Holly/pine = winter hospitality`, `steam curls = warmth`.

## 6) Moon-Moth Nocturne Portrait
**Prompt**  
`Nocturnal Art Nouveau portrait of an androgynous figure beneath a crescent moon, moon-moths orbiting an opalescent halo, deep indigo and silver palette, flowing hair lines echo moth wing curves, stained-glass segmentation in the background only, soft velvety light, intricate but restrained filigree border, poetic Belle Époque illustration mood, 4:5`  
**Rationale**: Emphasizes atmospheric elegance while keeping composition clean.  
**Motif mapping**: `Moths = transformation`, `crescent = intuition/night`.

## 7) River Spirit Panel
**Prompt**  
`Decorative Art Nouveau panel of a river spirit, elongated figure emerging from curling water reeds, serpentine line rhythm, repeating water-lily modules, turquoise, moss, and pearl palette, embossed ornamental frame, enamel and ceramic glaze surface feel, balanced negative space, artisan decorative panel style, 1:1 format`  
**Rationale**: Strong for tile/panel concepts where repeat motifs matter.  
**Motif mapping**: `Water-lily = serenity`, `reeds = continuity/flow`.

## 8) Allegory of Industry and Spring
**Prompt**  
`Complex Art Nouveau allegory uniting Industry and Spring: two personified figures, one with gear-and-vine scepter, one with blossom garland, interlaced whiplash curves connecting factory skyline and flowering meadow, muted brass, sage, and rose palette, structured tripartite composition, ornamental border with symbolic corner medallions, elegant lithograph finish, 3:2`  
**Rationale**: Demonstrates dual-theme symbolism without abandoning decorative cohesion.  
**Motif mapping**: `Gear+vine = technology with nature`, `triptych = conceptual balance`.

## 9) Opera Night Poster
**Prompt**  
`Art Nouveau opera night poster, soprano in profile with flowing scarf becoming musical staves, laurel and lily ornament frame, flat color blocks in burgundy, ivory, and muted gold, high-contrast silhouette, reserved lower cartouche for event details, vintage paper texture, refined line economy, 2:3`  
**Rationale**: Useful for event visuals needing both elegance and readable structure.  
**Motif mapping**: `Laurel = achievement/art`, `musical scarf = voice made visible`.

## 10) Four Seasons Allegorical Tableau
**Prompt**  
`Art Nouveau allegorical tableau of the Four Seasons as four interconnected figures in a circular composition, each framed by symbolic flora (spring cherry blossom, summer sunflower, autumn vine leaves, winter hellebore), continuous flowing line network, stained-glass inspired background sectors, harmonious jewel-muted palette, ornate ring border, storybook mural quality, 1:1`  
**Rationale**: Built for ensemble compositions with clear symbolic segmentation.  
**Motif mapping**: `Seasonal flowers = cyclical time`, `circular layout = eternal return`.
