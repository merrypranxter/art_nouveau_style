# Base Prompt Templates (Art Nouveau)

Use these as modular scaffolds. Replace bracketed fields with your subject, mood, and medium constraints.

## 1) Portrait Template

**Template**

`[SUBJECT], [AGE/IDENTITY], in an Art Nouveau portrait, three-quarter view, flowing hair and organic linework, ornamental halo motif inspired by whiplash curves, botanical framing with [FLOWER/PLANT], muted jewel palette ([COLOR 1], [COLOR 2], [COLOR 3]), elegant contour emphasis, decorative yet balanced composition, fine lithographic texture, era-appropriate styling, high visual harmony, [LIGHTING STYLE], [MEDIUM LOOK], [ASPECT RATIO].`

**Optional style add-ons**
- sinuous line emphasis
- stained-glass segmentation in background only
- metallic filigree accents (subtle)
- symbol motif for character trait: `[SYMBOL]`

---

## 2) Poster Template

**Template**

`Art Nouveau poster design for [TOPIC/EVENT/PRODUCT], central figure [SUBJECT/ACTION], strong silhouette, arabesque border system, flat decorative color fields, elegant frame cartouche reserved for text, hand-printed lithograph feel, limited palette of [3–5 COLORS], rhythmic floral repeats with [MOTIF], period-authentic ornament density, high legibility zones, [VERTICAL/HORIZONTAL] composition, [ASPECT RATIO].`

**Optional style add-ons**
- parchment paper grain
- vintage ink registration imperfection (very subtle)
- cameo medallions and corner ornaments
- restrained gold-ink simulation

---

## 3) Decorative Panel Template

**Template**

`Decorative Art Nouveau panel depicting [THEME], symmetrical or near-symmetrical layout, elongated figure(s) integrated with vine geometry, repeated botanical modules ([PLANT TYPE]), flowing contour lines, enamel-like flat tones with selective gradients, ornamental border and inner frame, mosaic/stained-glass influence, refined negative space control, crafted decorative object aesthetic, [LIGHTING], [ASPECT RATIO].`

**Optional style add-ons**
- triptych segmentation
- ceramic glaze surface finish
- tapestry-like weave texture
- embossed line simulation

---

## 4) Allegorical Scene Template

**Template**

`Allegorical Art Nouveau scene representing [ABSTRACT IDEA/SEASON/ELEMENT], personified as [FIGURE TYPE], surrounded by symbolic flora and fauna ([SYMBOL SET]), sweeping whiplash forms connecting foreground and background, layered decorative depth, dreamy but readable narrative composition, turn-of-the-century poster/illustration synthesis, restrained atmospheric perspective, poetic color script ([PALETTE NOTES]), intricate but coherent ornament language, [ASPECT RATIO].`

**Optional style add-ons**
- zodiac/celestial icon ring
- seasonal emblem set in corners
- symbolic object in hand: `[OBJECT]`
- border inscription area (leave blank)
